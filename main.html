History

Main articles: History of blogging and online diary

The term weblog was coined by Jorn Barger on December 17, 1997. In 1999, Peter Merholz shortened it to "blog" by splitting "we blog." Later, Evan Williams at Pyra Labs popularized the term "blogger."

Origins

Before blogs, online communities existed as Usenet, GEnie, BIX, CompuServe, email lists, and Bulletin Board Systems (BBS).
