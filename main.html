History of Blogging

Main articles: History of blogging and online diary

The term "weblog" was coined by Jorn Barger on December 17, 1997. Later, in 1999, Peter Merholz shortened it to "blog" by breaking it into "we blog." Soon after, Evan Williams at Pyra Labs popularized the terms "blog" and "blogger."

Origins

Before blogging, online communities existed as Usenet, GEnie, BIX, CompuServe, email lists, and Bulletin Board Systems (BBS).
